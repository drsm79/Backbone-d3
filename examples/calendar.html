Calendar

This is a port of the calendar example from the d3 pages. The data used here is summarised FAA plane crash data from data.gov that I got from Mike Miller of Cloudant. I munged it up so it's just date:number of crashes. The data is unsorted. See calendar.json for the details. Days are coloured by the ratio of crashes to total number of crashes in the data set.
